A full-day workshop (~7 hours) combining invited talks, a poster session with short-paper teaser talks, and a structured synthesis discussion.

Expected outcomes
A cross-domain mapping of interaction design patterns that support behavior change across embodied and virtual agents
A shared set of evaluation recommendations for behavior-change-oriented HRI/HAI studies
A focused ethics checklist for persuasive/behavior-change agents presented at RO-MAN
A brief post-workshop summary report capturing a community roadmap and open research questions
